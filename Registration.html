About

Welcome to the official Team Titans Selection Challenge for ISRO Robotics Challenge 2026! In this challenge, you will be given a problem statement along with curated learning resources to guide your journey. The selection process is designed to test your skills, problem-solving ability, creativity, consistency, and passion for robotics. After completing this challenge, shortlisted candidates will be invited to join Team Titans.

How to Participate

  • Fill the Registration Form: Click the provided Register button for Registration for the challenge.
  • Choose Your Domain: Select your preferred domain: Mechanical, Electronics, Software or Media.
  • Access the Problem Statement: Download the domain-specific problem statement using the provided button on the top.
  • Access the Learning Resources: Use the curated learning resources to guide your progress throughout the challenge(provided in PS button).

What We're Looking For

  • Consistency and dedication
  • Strong problem-solving skills
  • Creativity and innovation
  • Ability to learn independently
  • Teamwork and communication
